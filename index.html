About Club

Martial arts club, part of the MMA Union of Russia, signs contracts with UFC, BELLATOR, OUN FC. The main directions of the GOR MMA martial arts school are ethics fight, freestyle wrestling, sambo, boxing, crossfit, kickboxing, judo.
Young and ambitious coaches, masters of their craft, will help each visitor to the club in achieving high results and personal victories. We will give you a chance to become a real champion, help you prepare for competitions and tournaments. Classes with us are a great opportunity to improve health, gain self-confidence, "pull up" physically and grow mentally.
